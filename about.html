About SpinnahApp

Creating native macOS applications that feel at home on your Mac

Our Philosophy

I create native macOS applications that I genuinely want to use. When I can't find exactly what I need, or when existing cross-platform solutions feel out of place on macOS, I build it myself.

My apps are designed specifically for macOS, taking advantage of native features and following Apple's design principles. This means they feel at home on your Mac rather than like ports from other platforms.

🔒 Privacy First

I don't collect any information from you. My apps run entirely on your devices and don't phone home. Some apps may offer optional web functionality through APIs that you would obtain directly from data providers, but this is completely optional—you can use the apps without any external connections if you prefer. Any API usage is strictly between you and the API provider.

Pricing Philosophy

For paid applications, I currently use a one-time purchase model. My intention is to maintain this approach and keep apps updated with each annual macOS release. I don't plan to move to subscription pricing unless I add features that create ongoing operational costs—and even then, existing functionality would remain available without requiring a subscription.

Please note: While I'm committed to supporting my apps, software development involves many variables, and this represents my current intentions rather than a binding commitment.
