Why I Created SpinnahApp

"When I can't find exactly what I need, or when existing solutions feel out of place on macOS, I build it myself."

SpinnahApp started from a simple frustration: too many cross-platform tools that don't feel native to macOS. As someone who spends hours every day on my Mac, I wanted tools that not only work well but feel like they belong here.

Each app I create is designed specifically for macOS, taking advantage of native features and following Apple's design principles. This means they feel at home on your Mac rather than like ports from other platforms.

Privacy-First App Development

"Your data should stay on your devices. Period."

In an era where every app seems to want to collect your data, I'm taking a different approach. All SpinnahApp products are designed with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought.

This means no tracking, no analytics, no data collection. Your files stay on your devices, and any optional web functionality uses APIs that you control directly. It's a simple philosophy: if I don't need your data to provide the service, I won't ask for it.
